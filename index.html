About Me


My name is Jared Sarabia, an analyst with a passion for data science. Officially, I am an analyst working in the public sector, analyzing and providing solutions for logistics problems, while also streamlining processes by integrating natural language processing into current business practices. Outside of work, I am building to my Master's of Science in Data Science at Northwestern University. Which is all a formal way of saying I'm a data enthusiast that's loving my explorations into this field.

This site acts as my repository for my projects, my resume, and any personal thoughts regarding data science and my personal journey through the field. Most projects are technical, as I learn certain methods or models, while others are explorations into my interests such as food waste, climate change, basketball, and rock climbing.
